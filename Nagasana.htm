Nagasana

Region

Back muscles

Target Muscles

  1. Trapezius/Rhomboid
  2. Erector spinae
  3. Quadratus Lumborum

Pre requisites

Chadhuranga Astanga Namaskar

Steps to achieve

Step 1: Achieve the Chadhuranga Astanga Namaskar.

Step 2: Pushing the palms to the floor, lift the chest off the floor. Note: Make sure the elbows bent (Nagasana-1)

Step 3: Compress the Middle back bending backward. Note: It is necessary to keep the elbows close to the body.

Note: It is also necessary to activate the scapula. If you push the shoulders down, the scapula will be activated.

Step 4: Come to Nagasana-2 compressing lower-back.

Note: If you have sharp pain in the lower back. That maybe SI joint pain. The solution to that is pigeon pose that stretches the Gluteus maximus.

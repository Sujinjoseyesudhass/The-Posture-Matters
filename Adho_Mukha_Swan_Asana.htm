Adho Mukha Swan Asana

Region

Lower Leg

Target Muscles

Calf

Pre requisites

Parsvottan asana
Ashwa Sanchalan asana
Trigonasana
Pigeon

Steps to achieve

Step 1: Stand comfortably.
Step 2: Bend down and touch the floor.
Step 3: Bring the legs backward and come to the triagle position as in the image.
Step 4: Rock back and forth with the shoulders; feel the stretch in the calf muscles.

Ashwa Sanchalan asana

Region

Hip

Target Muscles

  • Iliacus
  • Quadriceps

Pre requisites

Chadhuranga Astanga Namaskar

Steps to achieve

Step 1: Stand comfortably.
Step 2: Bring your left leg forward and place your hands in the sides of the left leg.
Step 3: Feel the stretch in the target muscles.
(Do the same work out for the right-leg.)

Note: This stretch will first work on the iliocus; If you feel a sharp stretch in the iliacus, that means the Quadriceps is not working along with Iliocus due to sedentary life style. It is advisable to focus on Quadriceps stretches: standing, kneeling, and twisted.

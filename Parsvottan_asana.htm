Parsvottan asana

Region

Thighs

Target Muscles

Hamstring

Pre requisites

Ashwa Sanchalan asana
Trigonasana
Pigeon

Steps to achieve

Step 1: Stand comfortably.
Step 2: Spread the legs 3 feet apart.
Step 3: Turn the right leg sideways.
Step 4: Hold the hands behind the back.
Step 5: Lean down over the right leg.

Note: It is not necessary to overlap the chest on the thighs. Go as far as you feel a comfortable pain. The Hamstrings are usually so tight in sedentary life style. So be ready to feel the stretch in 3 different portions as shown in the image. Every day or two, you will feel the pain in any one of the portions: lower, mid, or upper.


(Do the same for the left leg.)

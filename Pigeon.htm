Pigeon

Region

Hip and Legs

Target Muscles

•Gluteus maximus
•Abductor & Adductor

Pre requisites

Ashwa Sanchalan asana

Steps to achieve

Step 1: Come to Push-up position.
Step 2: Bring the working leg forward with the heel close to the pelvis.
Step 3: Lean forward to the floor to feel the stretch in the Gluteus maximus muscles.

Note: You may feel a stetch in the iliacus also while doing this exercise. But remember the main goal here is to stretch the Gluteus Maximus.

Step 4: Bring the working leg a little away carefully.
Step 5: Lean forward and feel the stretch in the Abductor & Adductor muscles.
